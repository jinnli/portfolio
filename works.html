Photography

I got my first camera, a SONY mini SLR camera, as my 12th birthday present. Over the years, I have travelled to 32 Chinese provinces and 2 countries outside China. I would always bring my camera with me on those trips and use it to capture moments I find beautiful or interesting. Street is my favirote theme of photography, because I love observing the details in society and keeping them in my photos. In the summer of 2022, my SONY camera broke down during my trip to Xinjiang province, so I switched to another mini SLR camera made by Olympus and have been using it ever since.

I've picked out about 50 of my best photos for you to see. To make things easier, here are some maps directing you to the pictures I've taken in different parts of the world. You can also view the pictures by their categories if you want to.
